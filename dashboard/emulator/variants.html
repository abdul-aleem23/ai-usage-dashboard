ESP32 HUD Style Variants

Alternative 320x240 landscape designs. The original version remains in index.html.

Target: CYD 320x240 / ILI9341

01 UAC Status Panel

A harder industrial HUD: dense, metal-framed, high contrast, most similar to the first version but more ordered.

02 Hellforge Warning Board

Warmer Doom-style palette with amber/red accents, heavy warning strips, and a chunkier arcade feel.

03 Neon CRT Terminal

More retro-computer than Doom: cyan/green glow, black terminal layout, and tighter system-monitor rows.

04 Cyberpunk NetRunner

Neon magenta/cyan tactical terminal: saturated, sharp, and more futuristic while keeping the segmented meter language.

05 Orbital Command HUD

Military sci-fi console: olive armor panels, amber/cyan telemetry, and a cleaner command-deck feel.
